About

Dr. Timothy Usman is professional with strong academic background in accounting.Over time, he has developed intense passion for leadership development and has consequently invested time and energy in leadership training and mentorship. One of these efforts in his involvement in Maxwell leadership in the united states where he has been certified Team Member (MLCT) with unlimited access to continous leadership development and mentorship. He has also been actively involved in Global leadership summits. He has also been part of national institute for policy and strategic studies, kuru, Nigeria, and an alumnus of BetterWays Consulting & coaching Academy. As a senior manager in the public sector as well as long term investment in several leadership programmes , he has acquired the skills and insights needed to navigate complex leadership challenges in today's dynamic environment. His interest in leadership is anchored on passion to see everyone leading and being led well and leveraging vast knowledge and experience to inspire and guide others in ledership roles. Whether through mentorship, workshops, or speaking engagements, he is dedicated to fostering effective and ethical leadership practices He believes in giving back to the society and has since been involved in various intiatives aimed at promoting empowerment and leadership development of individuals with the goal of making sure that "Everyone wins when the leader gets better".
